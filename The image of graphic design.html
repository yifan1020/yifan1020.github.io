The Image of Graphic Design

During Linda Van Deursen three-week workshop, she encouraged us to reflect on graphic design elements that are currently prevalent on digital platforms. These images show an ideal, untouched, and immaculate version of graphic design.

Is it important whether these objects really exist?
Can this image of graphic design be challenged?
Can the image of graphic design be used to other ends?
Can it be tested and pushed?
Can it be undermined, corrupted, or subverted?

I was guided and inspired by her to reproduce the four tailoring lines of Margiela on the surfaces of objects.
