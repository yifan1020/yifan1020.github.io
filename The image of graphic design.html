The Image of Graphic Design

The Image of Graphic Design

Video:"Tu Wei" Love Wrods


Prompt: We currently mostly encounter elements of graphic design on digital platforms. These images show an ideal, untouched and immaculate version of graphic design. What was once a graphic design object is now a dummy, a mock-up in a photomontage or a rendering. Is it important whether these objects really exist? Can this image of graphic design be challenged? Can the image of graphic design be used to other ends? Can it be tested and pushed? Can it be undermined, corrupted, or subverted? During a three-week workshop with my idol Linda Van Deursen, I was guided and inspired by her to reproduce the four tailoring lines of Margiela on the surfaces of objects.
