Somatic Writing

This is a writing exercise that explores how to express vision based on the form of language. The writing practice that interweaves between art and design has always been the part that interests me the most. Because whether we write or read, our visual practices are inevitably rooted in language. We can think of writing as a form of painting.

How do site-specific and predetermined circumstances shape my writing? Use writing as a visual exercise to move beyond the printed form.

The practice condition of this body writing is that I isolate myself in my room, observe my internal and external world, and talk to the objects in the room.
