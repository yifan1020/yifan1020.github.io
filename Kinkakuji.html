A Little Book: Kinkakuji

This is a typography exercise with text taken from my favorite novel: The Temple of the Golden Pavilion.

The text face is Futura. This is a geometric sans-serif typeface designed by Paul Renner and released in 1927. It was designed as a contribution to the New Frankfurt project. It is based on geometric shapes, especially the circle, similar in spirit to the Bauhaus design style of the period.
