"Tu Wei" Love Words

Then it was derived from "Too Cool" fashion: Tu Wei Love Words. This kind of mass-carnival-like language game is actually a study of mass spiritual life, social group behavior, and the trend of popular culture.
- The Social Media Research Center of Beijing University

"The term '土味' generally refers to language, actions or behaviors that do not conform to trends and are relatively low-level, but in '土味' love words, '土味' often refers to down-to-earth and simple language expressions.

In my opinion, these language expressions are more of a humor than a real way to convey love to one's partner. I created a text-based video of these '土味' love words to express this cultural phenomenon."
