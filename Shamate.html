Shan Shui Hair

While working on a book about “To Cool” fashion, I noticed Shamate. They are migrant workers who like to get attention with exaggerated hairstyles. I used their hair as the main body as a collage, and then it seemed that these hairs grew out of this landscape.

Because this group initially wanted to be seen, but in the end, the public's disgust and antipathy towards them made them hide. So it's like this brightly colored hair is looming in this landscape painting.
