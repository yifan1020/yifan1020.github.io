“Too Cool” Fashion

This is a bilingual book about "tu ku" fashion, which can be translated as "rustic" or "tacky" fashion. This term refers to a fashion trend that originated in China and celebrates a bold, colorful, and sometimes kitschy aesthetic.

While working on this book, I felt conflicted because this is a style I am afraid to try and may not be what I aspire to in terms of my aesthetic awareness. However, I was moved by the spirit of this local, free, and unfettered fashion that does not follow others' trends.

I selected 14 articles out of hundreds of reports on this phenomenon to create this book. To distinguish between the Chinese and English texts, I placed the images in the middle. This is the first time I have made a thread-bound book, and I find it very enjoyable.
